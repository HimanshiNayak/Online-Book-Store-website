Memories of the sword

1. Overview:

A self-help book offers a comprehensive approach to crafting a fulfilling career that aligns with your values, interests, and strengths. Authors Bill Burnett and Dave Evans, known for their work in design thinking and career development, provide actionable insights and strategies to help readers navigate the complexities of modern work life. Through practical exercises and frameworks, the book empowers individuals to redefine success on their own terms, fostering greater satisfaction and balance in both professional and personal spheres.

2. About the author:

John Myers was born in Seattle in 1972. Always passionate about the human mind, at the age of 19, he won a scholarship to the prestigious English University of Oxford, where he graduated with honors in psychology.
